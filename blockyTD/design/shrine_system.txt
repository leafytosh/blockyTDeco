SHRINE SYSTEM

The shrine is a central mechanic in blockyTD.

States:
1. Dormant
- No effects
- Towers use normal emotes

2. Awakening
- Visual ripple
- Towers shift to O_O
- Enemies shift to >_<

3. Active
- Towers gain buffs
- Emotes: ÷w<
- Enemies become more aggressive
- Emotes: ( ͡° ʖ̯ ͡° )

4. Fading
- Buffs weaken
- Towers show T_T
- Enemies show >w< (escape attempt)

Shrine triggers:
- Wave milestones
- Player emotes in chat
- Tartar lore events
