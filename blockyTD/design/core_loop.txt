The core loop of blockyTD:

1. Player places towers on designated tiles.
2. Waves of enemies spawn and follow a path.
3. Towers attack enemies automatically.
4. Enemies express ASCII emotions based on their state.
5. Player earns currency from defeated enemies.
6. Player upgrades towers to increase power and unlock new emotes.
7. Shrine activates periodically, boosting towers and altering enemy behavior.
8. Chat system allows ASCII emotes to influence gameplay.
9. Survive all waves to win.

The loop is designed to be readable, expressive, and emotionally reactive.
